Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Daily Idol ("the App") is a personal-use webapp that fetches and displays curated K-pop idol images from Pinterest boards. This Privacy Policy explains what data is (and is not) collected and how it’s used.

2. Data We Collect

  • Pinterest Pins: Public image URLs, captions, and metadata fetched via the Pinterest API.
  • Favorites: If you “heart” an image, that ID is stored locally in your browser’s storage.
  • Technical Data: Server logs (timestamps, API request counts) stored on Firebase for maintenance and debugging.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To display daily idol images and related details.
  • To power the “favorites” feature locally in your browser.
  • To monitor app performance and troubleshoot errors via Firebase logs.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Pinterest API: Used to fetch public pins.
  • Firebase: Firestore for storing pin metadata; Cloud Functions for scheduled fetch jobs; Hosting for serving the site.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Daily Idol does not use cookies or analytics scripts. All data is either fetched on demand or stored locally in your browser (for favorites).

6. Your Choices

  • You can clear your browser’s local storage at any time to remove all saved “favorites.”
  • If you wish to revoke the app’s Pinterest access token, please remove the token in your Pinterest developer dashboard.

7. Security

We follow industry-standard practices to secure your data on Firebase and to protect our API keys. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.
